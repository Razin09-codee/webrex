About Cracked Screen

"Just a little prank to fool your friends!" - Cracked Screen

Cracked Screen is a playful application designed to simulate a cracked screen on your device. Perfect for pranking friends, this tool provides an amusing visual of a broken display, making it seem as though your device has suffered a mishap.

The app includes various realistic cracked screen images and sound effects that enhance the experience. It’s easy to use and can create a light-hearted atmosphere during gatherings or parties.

With its simple interface and engaging content, Cracked Screen is a fun way to enjoy harmless pranks and bring laughter to your social interactions.
