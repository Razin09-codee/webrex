About Fake Call

"Prank your friends with a simulated call!" - Fake Call

Fake Call is a fun tool that allows users to simulate phone calls for various purposes, whether for pranks or to escape awkward situations. With just a few taps, you can receive a call from anyone you choose.

This application provides customizable options, including the caller ID, ringtone, and call duration, making it a popular choice for users looking to add some humor to their day.

With an intuitive interface, Fake Call is easy to use, ensuring that users can create the perfect fake call experience every time.
