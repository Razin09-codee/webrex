About Amazon Kindle

"A reader lives a thousand lives before he dies." - George R.R. Martin

Amazon Kindle is a series of e-readers designed and marketed by Amazon. Launched in 2007, Kindle devices enable users to browse, buy, download, and read e-books, magazines, and other digital media through wireless connectivity.

Kindle's core products include Kindle Paperwhite, Kindle Oasis, and the Kindle app available on various devices. It also provides access to Amazon’s extensive library of e-books, audiobooks, and other digital content, as well as subscription services like Kindle Unlimited.

Amazon Kindle’s mission is to enhance the reading experience by providing easy access to digital content, making it convenient for readers to enjoy books anytime, anywhere.
