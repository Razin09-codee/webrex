About Google Books

"A room without books is like a body without a soul." - Marcus Tullius Cicero

Google Books is a service by Google that provides users with access to a vast library of digitized books. Launched in 2004, Google Books allows users to discover, preview, and in some cases, read full texts from a variety of genres and topics.

Key features of Google Books include searchable texts, previews, and the option to purchase or download books from partner retailers. Google Books has digitized millions of books, making literature more accessible to readers around the world.

Google Books’ mission is to make the world’s books discoverable online, supporting global learning and access to knowledge.
