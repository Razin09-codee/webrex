About Goodreads

"A good book is an event in my life." - Stendhal

Goodreads is the world’s largest site for readers and book recommendations, where people can discover new books, track their reading progress, and connect with other book enthusiasts. Founded in 2007, Goodreads hosts millions of user-generated reviews, ratings, and book lists.

Key features include personalized recommendations, reviews, community discussions, and the ability to create reading lists. Goodreads offers a platform for readers to engage with authors, participate in reading challenges, and join book clubs.

Goodreads’ mission is to help people discover and share books they love, creating a global community of book lovers and reviewers.
