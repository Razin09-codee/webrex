About Hoopla

"A library is not a luxury but one of the necessities of life." - Henry Ward Beecher

Hoopla is a digital media service that provides free access to a vast library of e-books, audiobooks, movies, and more, available to patrons through their local libraries. Hoopla makes borrowing digital content easy and accessible with no waitlists.

Key features include access to thousands of titles across genres, instant digital borrowing, and the ability to stream or download content for offline use. With partnerships with libraries, Hoopla allows users to enjoy a rich variety of digital content with just a library card.

Hoopla’s mission is to connect people to the entertainment and information they want, providing free access to a digital library for readers and audiences worldwide.
