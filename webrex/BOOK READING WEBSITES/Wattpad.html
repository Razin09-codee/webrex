About Wattpad

"The world is never the same once a good story has been added to it." - Unknown

Wattpad is a global storytelling platform where writers and readers connect to create and discover original stories. Founded in 2006, it has become a hub for amateur and professional writers alike, covering genres from romance to sci-fi.

Wattpad's key features include a community-driven library of user-generated stories, the opportunity for writers to reach millions of readers, and platforms like Wattpad Studios, which connects popular stories with multimedia production opportunities.
