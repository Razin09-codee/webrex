About BookBub

"A room without books is like a body without a soul." - Cicero

BookBub is a free service that helps readers discover new books through daily deals and recommendations across various genres and platforms. Known for delivering discounts on popular and lesser-known titles, BookBub connects readers with e-books that suit their interests and budgets.

Subscribers receive customized daily emails highlighting discounted or free e-books based on their preferences, with options available for Kindle, Nook, and other e-readers. BookBub also offers readers the chance to follow favorite authors for updates on new releases and special deals.

BookBub’s mission is to help readers find the best deals on great books, creating a bridge between authors and readers in the digital age.
