About Google

"Innovation distinguishes between a leader and a follower." - Steve Jobs

Google is a multinational technology company primarily known for its search engine, which is the most widely used in the world. Founded in 1998 by Larry Page and Sergey Brin, it has expanded its services to include advertising technologies, cloud computing, software, and hardware.

Key products include Gmail, Google Drive, Google Maps, and the Android operating system. Google is a subsidiary of Alphabet Inc., which was created as part of a corporate restructuring in 2015. The company is also known for its innovations in artificial intelligence and machine learning.

Google’s mission is to organize the world’s information and make it universally accessible and useful.
