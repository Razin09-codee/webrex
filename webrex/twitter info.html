About Twitter

"What's happening?" - Twitter

Twitter is a microblogging platform founded in 2006 by Jack Dorsey, Biz Stone, Evan Williams, and Noah Glass. It allows users to post and interact with messages known as "tweets," which are limited to 280 characters. With its real-time updates, Twitter has become a key platform for news, trends, and discussions.

Users can follow others, engage through likes, retweets, and replies, and use hashtags to connect with broader conversations. Twitter is widely used by individuals, businesses, and organizations to share information, promote content, and engage with their audience.

As a significant player in social media, Twitter plays a vital role in shaping public discourse and providing a platform for global conversations.
