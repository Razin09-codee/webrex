About Roblox

"Powering Imagination." - Roblox

Roblox is a global platform that brings people together through play, offering millions of user-created games and experiences. Launched in 2006, Roblox has grown into one of the most popular online platforms for both playing and creating games.

With its easy-to-use game creation tools, Roblox Studio enables users to design, build, and share games with a worldwide audience. The platform fosters creativity and collaboration among its vast community, making it a unique space for creators and players alike.

Roblox continues to expand its universe, aiming to empower users with the tools to create immersive virtual experiences.
