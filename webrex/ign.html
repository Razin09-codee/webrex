About IGN

"Entertainment is a passion, and so is sharing it." - IGN

IGN is one of the world’s leading sources for gaming, tech, entertainment news, and reviews. Launched in 1996, it has become a major platform for gamers, movie buffs, and tech enthusiasts, offering insightful reviews, previews, and in-depth articles.

With a vast library of video content, guides, and community features, IGN brings audiences the latest from the gaming world and beyond. It’s the go-to place for up-to-date news, covering everything from blockbuster games to indie releases.

IGN remains a trusted voice in the industry, keeping fans informed, entertained, and connected to the ever-evolving world of entertainment and gaming.
