About Yandex

"Innovation distinguishes between a leader and a follower." - Steve Jobs

Yandex is a Russian multinational corporation founded in 1997, primarily known for its search engine, which is the most popular in Russia. The company offers a wide range of services, including maps, translation, cloud storage, and online advertising.

Yandex is also involved in developing technologies in artificial intelligence, self-driving cars, and smart home devices.

With a strong focus on the Russian-speaking market, it has expanded its services to various countries and is often referred to as "Russia's Google."
