About Battle.net

"Connecting gamers worldwide." - Battle.net

Battle.net is Blizzard Entertainment’s online gaming platform, connecting players with iconic games like World of Warcraft, Overwatch, Diablo, and Hearthstone. Established in 1996, it was one of the first services to offer online matchmaking and multiplayer capabilities, making it a pioneer in the gaming industry.

With an active community, regular updates, and cross-game features, Battle.net provides a seamless experience for gamers to connect, compete, and collaborate. It remains a hub for Blizzard's diverse player base, offering integrated services and a unified launcher for all Blizzard games.

Battle.net aims to foster community, camaraderie, and competitive play, all while continuing to innovate in online gaming.
