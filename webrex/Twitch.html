About Twitch

"Your entertainment, your community." - Twitch

Twitch is the world’s leading live streaming platform for gamers and content creators. Launched in 2011, Twitch has become a global community where millions of people come together to watch, chat, and interact over shared interests.

Known for its focus on gaming, Twitch also offers streams in music, art, talk shows, and more. The platform enables creators to build communities and interact with fans in real-time, enhancing viewer engagement.

Twitch’s mission is to empower creators and bring people together, making it the go-to platform for live streaming and digital content.
