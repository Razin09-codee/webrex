About Homicide Monitor

"Informed insights into the world’s most pressing violence issues." - Homicide Monitor

Homicide Monitor is a comprehensive platform designed to track, analyze, and provide statistics on global homicide rates and trends. Developed by experts in crime analysis and public safety, this tool offers real-time data on the prevalence and patterns of homicide across various regions.

Our platform is committed to helping researchers, policymakers, and the public understand and address the complex factors driving homicide rates. Through interactive maps, detailed statistics, and insightful reports, Homicide Monitor sheds light on this critical issue facing communities worldwide.

Stay informed with Homicide Monitor and explore the data to understand the global impact of violence.
