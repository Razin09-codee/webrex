About Shut Up and Go to Bed

"The monsters under your bed are waiting..." - Shut Up and Go to Bed

"Shut Up and Go to Bed" is a collection of dark bedtime tales crafted to unsettle and unnerve. These aren’t the bedtime stories you’d want to hear alone at night. From chilling ghost stories to eerie mysteries, each story is designed to keep you on edge.

The tales explore nightmares, nocturnal fears, and spine-chilling legends passed down through whispers and shadows. If you're brave enough to read before sleep, prepare yourself — these stories are meant to stay with you long after the lights go out.

Turn off the lights, lie down, and let "Shut Up and Go to Bed" lead you into a world where sleep is the last thing you’ll find...
