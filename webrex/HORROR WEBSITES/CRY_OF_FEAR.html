About Cry of Fear

"Enter the mind of fear..." - Cry of Fear

"Cry of Fear" is a psychological survival horror game that thrusts players into a nightmarish world filled with fear, isolation, and terror. Originally released as a Half-Life mod, it has since gained a cult following due to its eerie atmosphere, disturbing enemies, and immersive storyline.

Set in a dark and twisted urban environment, players navigate through claustrophobic streets and buildings, solving puzzles and fighting off grotesque creatures. Each step is a struggle for survival, pushing the player’s courage to the limit.

Prepare yourself for an unforgettable experience of horror and suspense with "Cry of Fear" — if you dare.
