About Shudder

"For fans of fear, thrills, and chills." - Shudder

Shudder is a premium streaming service dedicated to horror, thriller, and supernatural genres, offering a wide selection of exclusive films, series, and original content. It's the ultimate destination for fans seeking the thrill of horror.

With a curated library that spans cult classics, indie gems, and new releases, Shudder is known for its commitment to horror enthusiasts. The platform also offers live events, curated collections, and expert recommendations.

Shudder’s mission is to create a community for horror lovers, providing the scares and thrills that fans crave, available anytime, anywhere.
