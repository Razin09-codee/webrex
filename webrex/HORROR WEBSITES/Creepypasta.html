About Creepypasta

"Beware the tales that haunt your sleep..." - Creepypasta

"Creepypasta" is an online phenomenon that started as horror-themed stories and legends spread across forums, quickly gaining popularity as chilling tales of the internet. These user-created stories range from eerie ghost encounters to disturbing urban legends that leave readers questioning what's real.

Well-known characters like Slender Man, Jeff the Killer, and The Rake have emerged from Creepypasta lore, terrifying readers and becoming part of internet culture. Creepypasta stories have since evolved into a community for horror lovers, offering spine-tingling tales and supernatural lore.

Enter the dark world of Creepypasta, where every story could be more than just fiction...
