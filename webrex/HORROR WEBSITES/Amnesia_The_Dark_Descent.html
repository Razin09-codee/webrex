About Amnesia: The Dark Descent

"The last remaining light flickers away..." - Amnesia: The Dark Descent

"Amnesia: The Dark Descent" is a first-person survival horror game created by Frictional Games, celebrated for its intense atmosphere, immersive storytelling, and relentless sense of dread. Set in a dark, twisted castle, players must solve puzzles and avoid horrifying monsters as they uncover the protagonist’s terrifying past.

The game is renowned for its psychological horror, focusing on a sense of vulnerability and isolation. With limited light and resources, players must manage their sanity, while staying hidden from the horrors lurking in the shadows.

Step into the darkness with "Amnesia: The Dark Descent" — if you dare to remember what’s forgotten.
