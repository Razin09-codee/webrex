About State Bank of India (SBI)

"The banker to every Indian." - SBI

State Bank of India (SBI) is the largest public sector bank in India, with a rich history and a strong presence across the country and globally. Established in 1955, SBI has been a cornerstone of the Indian banking system, serving millions of customers with a wide range of financial products and services.

SBI offers services including savings and current accounts, loans, credit cards, investments, and insurance. The bank is committed to digital transformation, providing advanced online and mobile banking facilities to enhance customer experience.

With a mission to foster economic growth and empower individuals and businesses, SBI continues to play a pivotal role in the financial landscape of India.
