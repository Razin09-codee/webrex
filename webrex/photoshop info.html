About Photoshop

"The best way to predict the future is to create it." - Peter Drucker

Adobe Photoshop is a powerful image editing software that is widely used for graphic design, digital art, and photo manipulation. First released in 1988 by Thomas and John Knoll, it has since become the industry standard for raster graphics editing.

Photoshop offers a vast array of tools and features for retouching photos, creating digital paintings, and designing graphics for web and print. Its capabilities range from basic adjustments, such as cropping and color correction, to advanced techniques like compositing and digital painting.

Photoshop is part of Adobe's Creative Cloud, allowing users to collaborate and share their work seamlessly across various platforms.
