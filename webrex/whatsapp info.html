About WhatsApp

"Simple. Secure. Reliable messaging." - WhatsApp

WhatsApp is a widely-used messaging application founded in 2009 by Brian Acton and Jan Koum. It allows users to send text messages, voice messages, photos, and videos over the internet, enabling seamless communication across the globe.

Key features include end-to-end encryption, voice and video calls, group chats, and status updates, making it a versatile tool for personal and professional communication. WhatsApp has revolutionized the way people connect, providing a fast and secure platform for sharing information.

As a subsidiary of Facebook Inc. since 2014, WhatsApp continues to innovate, introducing new features to enhance user experience while maintaining a focus on privacy and security.
