About Zee5

"Entertainment at your fingertips." - Zee5

Zee5 is a popular streaming platform offering a diverse range of movies, TV shows, web series, and original content in multiple languages. Launched in 2018, Zee5 caters to a global audience with a rich library that includes regional content from India and international titles.

With a subscription, users can enjoy access to a variety of genres, including drama, comedy, romance, and action. Zee5 also features live TV channels and a selection of kids' programming, making it a comprehensive entertainment destination for all ages.

The platform's mission is to provide high-quality content and a seamless viewing experience, ensuring that users have access to their favorite entertainment anytime, anywhere.
