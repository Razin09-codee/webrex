About Prime Video

"The future of entertainment is here." - Amazon

Prime Video is a leading streaming service offered by Amazon that provides a vast library of movies, TV shows, documentaries, and original content. Launched in 2006, Prime Video is available to Amazon Prime members and features an extensive selection of titles across various genres.

Viewers can enjoy exclusive shows like "The Marvelous Mrs. Maisel," "The Boys," and "The Wheel of Time," along with a wide range of films and popular series. Prime Video also allows users to rent or purchase additional content not included in the subscription.

The mission of Prime Video is to deliver an unparalleled entertainment experience, allowing subscribers to access high-quality content anytime, anywhere, on a variety of devices.
