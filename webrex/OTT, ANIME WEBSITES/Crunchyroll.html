About Crunchyroll

"Your home for anime." - Crunchyroll

Crunchyroll is a premier streaming service specializing in anime, manga, and Asian dramas. Launched in 2006, Crunchyroll has grown into a vital hub for anime fans worldwide, offering a vast library of titles across various genres.

With simulcasts of the latest anime series directly from Japan, Crunchyroll provides viewers access to a diverse range of shows, including popular titles like "Naruto," "Attack on Titan," and "My Hero Academia." In addition to anime, the platform offers manga reading options and exclusive content for subscribers.

Crunchyroll's mission is to bring the best of Asian entertainment to global audiences, fostering a community of fans who share a passion for anime and culture.
