About Disney

"It all started with a mouse." - Walt Disney

The Walt Disney Company, commonly known as Disney, is a global entertainment conglomerate renowned for its animated films, iconic characters, and theme parks. Founded in 1923 by Walt Disney and Roy O. Disney, the company has become synonymous with family entertainment and creativity.

Disney's portfolio includes beloved animated classics like "Snow White and the Seven Dwarfs," "The Lion King," and "Frozen," as well as successful franchises such as Marvel, Star Wars, and Pixar. In addition to films, Disney operates theme parks around the world, providing immersive experiences for fans of all ages.

Disney's mission is to entertain, inform, and inspire people around the globe through the power of storytelling and imagination.
