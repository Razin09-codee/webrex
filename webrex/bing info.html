About Bing

"Innovation distinguishes between a leader and a follower." - Steve Jobs

Bing is a web search engine developed by Microsoft, launched in 2009 as a successor to Live Search. It aims to deliver a comprehensive search experience with features like image and video search, maps, and local business listings

Bing integrates with Microsoft services, including Office and Cortana, enhancing productivity and user convenience. The search engine also offers a rewards program, allowing users to earn points for searches and activities.

With a focus on user-friendly design and improved algorithms, Bing continues to evolve, striving to compete with other leading search engines like Google.
