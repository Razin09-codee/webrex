About Ask

"The best way to find answers is to ask questions."

Ask is a question-and-answer platform that allows users to seek information, share knowledge, and connect with a community of learners. Launched in 1996, it has evolved into a valuable resource for finding answers to a wide range of questions.

Users can ask questions on various topics, and the community provides answers based on their expertise and experience. The platform fosters collaboration and encourages the sharing of knowledge in a user-friendly environment.

Ask’s mission is to empower individuals by making information accessible and enabling informed decision-making through community engagement.
