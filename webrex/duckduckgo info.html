About DuckDuckGo

"Privacy, simplified." - DuckDuckGo

DuckDuckGo is a privacy-focused search engine founded in 2008 by Gabriel Weinberg. It is known for not tracking users or personalizing search results, emphasizing data protection and user privacy.

DuckDuckGo aggregates results from various sources and offers features like bang shortcuts for quick searches across other sites. It has gained popularity as an alternative to traditional search engines among users concerned about data privacy.

DuckDuckGo’s mission is to empower users to take control of their privacy online and to provide a safe browsing experience.
