About Endless Horse

"A horse is a horse, of course, but this one goes on forever!"

Endless Horse is a humorous website featuring an endlessly scrolling animation of a horse that seems to go on forever. It’s a simple yet entertaining experience that’s bound to make you smile.

The site allows you to scroll through a never-ending loop of the horse, providing a lighthearted and amusing distraction. It’s perfect for those moments when you just need a quick laugh or a break from the norm.

Visit Endless Horse to experience the joy of an infinite equine adventure!
