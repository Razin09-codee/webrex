About Zoom Quilt

"Embark on an infinite journey through mesmerizing worlds of art." - Zoom Quilt

Zoom Quilt is an endlessly zooming art project, a collaborative effort from multiple artists, offering an immersive and hypnotic visual experience. Each frame seamlessly connects to the next, creating a dreamlike world of infinite depth.

The Zoom Quilt website allows users to dive into this infinite art piece, where you can scroll endlessly through surreal, interconnected visuals. It’s a meditative experience that showcases the power of art collaboration and creativity.

Visit Zoom Quilt to embark on this endless visual journey and explore the wonders of boundless creativity.
