About Go (Golang)

Go, also known as Golang, is a statically typed programming language designed by Google. It is known for its simplicity, efficiency, and built-in concurrency support.

Go is widely used in system-level programming, cloud infrastructure, microservices, and web development. Its performance and scalability make it a top choice for large-scale applications.

Developers appreciate Go for its clean syntax, fast execution, and ability to handle concurrent tasks using goroutines.
