About Facebook

"It's not about what you like. It's about what you share." - Facebook

Facebook is a leading social media platform founded by Mark Zuckerberg, along with his college roommates, in 2004. It allows users to connect with friends and family, share updates, photos, and videos, and discover news and events.

Key features of Facebook include the News Feed, Groups, Events, and Marketplace, enabling users to engage in various ways. With billions of active users worldwide, Facebook has transformed how we communicate and interact online.

Facebook's mission is to give people the power to build community and bring the world closer together.
