About Facebook

"Connecting people." - Facebook

Facebook is a leading social media platform that enables users to connect, share, and communicate with others worldwide. Launched in 2004, it has grown to become one of the most influential platforms in the world.

With features like news feeds, groups, and messaging, Facebook allows users to stay informed about their friends and interests while providing businesses with tools for marketing and engagement.

As a pioneer in social networking, Facebook continues to innovate and expand its services, shaping the way people connect and communicate in the digital age.
