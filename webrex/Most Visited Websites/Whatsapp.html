About WhatsApp

"Simple. Secure. Reliable messaging." - WhatsApp

WhatsApp is a messaging application that allows users to send text messages, voice messages, make voice and video calls, and share images, documents, and other media. Launched in 2009, it has rapidly grown into one of the world's most popular communication platforms.

WhatsApp uses end-to-end encryption to ensure that conversations remain private and secure, making it a trusted choice for personal and group communication.

With its user-friendly interface and cross-platform availability, WhatsApp connects people globally, enabling seamless interaction and engagement.
