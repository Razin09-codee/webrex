About YouTube

"Broadcast yourself." - YouTube

YouTube is the world's largest video-sharing platform, enabling users to upload, view, and share videos across a wide range of genres. Founded in 2005, YouTube has grown into a global hub for content creators and audiences alike, hosting billions of videos and attracting millions of users daily.

The platform offers everything from music videos and tutorials to vlogs and documentaries, catering to diverse interests. YouTube also supports content creators through various monetization options, fostering a vibrant community of creators and viewers.

YouTube's mission is to give everyone a voice and show them the world, making it a vital space for entertainment, education, and expression.
