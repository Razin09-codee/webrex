About Wikipedia

"The free encyclopedia that anyone can edit." - Wikipedia

Wikipedia is a free online encyclopedia that allows users to collaboratively write, edit, and publish articles on a wide range of topics. Launched in 2001, it has grown to become one of the largest reference websites on the internet, providing millions of articles in various languages.

With a commitment to neutrality and verifiability, Wikipedia is maintained by a global community of volunteers, ensuring that information remains up-to-date and accessible to everyone.

As a valuable resource for learners and researchers alike, Wikipedia continues to expand its reach and impact, making knowledge available to people around the world.
