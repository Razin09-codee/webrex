About Yahoo

"The ultimate destination for news, entertainment, and more." - Yahoo

Yahoo is a web services provider that offers a variety of services, including news, email, finance, and search. Founded in 1994, Yahoo has been a pioneer in the internet space, providing users with a wide array of information and tools to navigate the online world.

Yahoo News delivers the latest headlines, while Yahoo Mail offers a popular email service with innovative features. The platform has evolved over the years to include a range of services that cater to the needs of its users.

As a major player in the digital landscape, Yahoo continues to serve millions of users globally, making information and connectivity accessible to everyone.
