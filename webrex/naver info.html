About Naver

"The search engine that knows you best." - Naver

Naver is a leading South Korean search engine and web portal, launched in 1999. It offers a wide range of services, including news, email, blogs, and a comprehensive search engine that provides tailored results based on user preferences.

Naver is known for its unique features, such as the Naver Dictionary, Naver Pay, and various community-driven platforms like Q&A services. It also integrates social media elements, allowing users to share content and connect with others.

Naver's mission is to provide users with convenient access to information and to facilitate knowledge sharing across its diverse platforms.
