About Telegram

"Telegram is a cloud-based mobile and desktop messaging app." - Telegram

Telegram is a cloud-based messaging app founded by Pavel Durov in 2013. It is known for its focus on speed and security, providing users with a seamless messaging experience across devices.

Telegram allows users to send messages, photos, videos, and files of any type, and create groups with up to 200,000 members. Its channels enable broadcasting to unlimited audiences, making it a powerful tool for communication and content sharing.

With strong encryption, Telegram prioritizes user privacy, offering features such as self-destructing messages and secret chats for enhanced security. The app has gained popularity worldwide, especially among users seeking secure and efficient communication methods.
