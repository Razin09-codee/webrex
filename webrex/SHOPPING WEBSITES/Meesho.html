About Meesho

"Empowering resellers to start their businesses." - Meesho

Meesho is a revolutionary online marketplace that allows individuals to start their own reselling businesses without any investment. Launched in 2015, it has become a popular platform for entrepreneurs across India.

With a wide range of products, including fashion, home goods, and accessories, Meesho provides resellers with easy access to quality products at wholesale prices, empowering them to sell via social media and other platforms.

Meesho is dedicated to transforming the e-commerce landscape by providing resources and support to resellers, ensuring they have the tools they need to succeed in their business ventures.
