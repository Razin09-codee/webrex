About Amazon

"Delivering smiles around the world." - Amazon

Amazon is a global e-commerce leader, offering a vast selection of products across categories like electronics, fashion, home essentials, and more. Founded in 1994, Amazon has set the standard for online shopping worldwide.

With its focus on customer satisfaction, Amazon provides fast delivery, reliable customer service, and convenient shopping options, making it a preferred choice for millions.

Amazon’s mission is to be Earth’s most customer-centric company, continually expanding its offerings and services to enhance the shopping experience for customers everywhere.
