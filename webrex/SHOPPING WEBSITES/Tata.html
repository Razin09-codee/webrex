About Tata Group

"We do what we do with a purpose." - Tata Group

The Tata Group is a global enterprise comprising over 100 companies operating in more than 100 countries across six continents. Founded in 1868, Tata is known for its commitment to quality, innovation, and sustainability.

With a diverse portfolio spanning multiple sectors, including steel, automobiles, IT, telecommunications, and consumer products, Tata has established itself as a leader in various industries.

Guided by its core values of integrity, excellence, and responsibility, Tata aims to contribute positively to the communities and environments in which it operates.
