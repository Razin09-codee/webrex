About Pepperfry

"Transform your home into a haven." - Pepperfry

Pepperfry is India's leading online marketplace for furniture and home decor, offering a wide range of products to enhance your living space. Established in 2012, it has revolutionized the way people shop for furniture in India.

With a focus on quality, design, and affordability, Pepperfry provides customers with an extensive selection of furniture, decor items, and more, ensuring that every home reflects the personality of its inhabitants.

Committed to excellent customer service, Pepperfry aims to make online shopping a delightful experience, with easy returns and a seamless delivery process.
