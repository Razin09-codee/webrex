Bob Saget

"I don't think I'm funny. I think I just say funny things."

Bob Saget was a beloved American comedian, actor, and television host best known for his role as Danny Tanner on the popular sitcom *Full House* and its sequel, *Fuller House*. Born on May 17, 1956, in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania, Saget started his career in stand-up comedy, gaining a reputation for his unique blend of humor that often mixed heartwarming moments with adult themes.

In addition to his television roles, Saget was a successful stand-up comedian, known for his raunchy humor, which contrasted sharply with his wholesome on-screen persona. He also hosted *America's Funniest Home Videos* from 1989 to 1997, further solidifying his status as a household name.

Sadly, Bob Saget passed away on January 9, 2022, leaving behind a legacy of laughter and love that continues to resonate with fans around the world.
