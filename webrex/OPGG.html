About OP.GG

"Analyze, improve, and climb the ranks." - OP.GG

OP.GG is a leading platform for real-time player statistics, builds, and guides, focusing on competitive games like League of Legends. Launched in 2012, it has become the go-to resource for players looking to analyze their performance and gain insights into the game.

Offering features like ranked leaderboards, champion guides, and match history analysis, OP.GG provides tools for players to improve their strategies and skills. The platform also covers other popular titles, including Overwatch, PUBG, and Valorant.

OP.GG is dedicated to supporting the gaming community with accurate and accessible data to enhance the player experience across various games.
