About Yahoo

"Innovation distinguishes between a leader and a follower." - Steve Jobs

Yahoo is a web services provider founded in 1994, originally known for its web directory and search engine. Over the years, it evolved into a comprehensive portal offering a variety of services, including email, news, finance, sports, and entertainment.

Yahoo was one of the pioneers of the early internet, but it struggled to keep pace with competitors like Google and Facebook. Despite facing challenges, Yahoo remains popular for its email service and news aggregation, and it continues to provide a range of digital content and services to users worldwide.

In 2017, Yahoo's core internet business was acquired by Verizon Communications and merged into Oath, a subsidiary now known as Yahoo Inc.
