About MSN

"Your daily source for news, sports, and entertainment." - MSN

MSN is a web portal and associated collection of Internet services and apps for Windows and mobile devices, provided by Microsoft. Launched in 1995, it offers users news, entertainment, sports, and lifestyle content from a variety of sources.

With its personalized homepage and seamless integration with Microsoft services, MSN allows users to access relevant news articles, videos, and more. The platform aims to keep users informed and entertained, providing a convenient way to stay updated on current events.

MSN is committed to delivering quality content and a user-friendly experience, making it a trusted source for millions of users worldwide.
