About The Times of India

"The world's largest selling English-language daily." - The Times of India

The Times of India (TOI) is one of India's most prominent and widely circulated English-language newspapers. Founded in 1838, TOI covers a diverse range of topics including national and international news, business, politics, sports, entertainment, and lifestyle.

With a rich history and a commitment to journalistic integrity, The Times of India is known for its in-depth reporting and analysis, providing readers with insightful perspectives on current events and cultural trends.

TOI aims to empower its audience through accurate information and a wide array of features, making it a trusted source for millions of readers across India and beyond.
