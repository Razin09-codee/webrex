About Fox News

"The most powerful name in news." - Fox News

Fox News is a leading American cable news network, known for its reporting, opinion programming, and analysis of current events. Launched in 1996, it has become one of the most influential news sources in the United States.

With a diverse range of programming, Fox News aims to provide viewers with timely news updates and in-depth analysis, covering politics, entertainment, and global events. The network is recognized for its unique blend of news and opinion journalism.

Fox News is committed to delivering news that is fair, balanced, and honest, making it a trusted source for millions of viewers across the country.
