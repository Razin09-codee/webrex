About Snapchat

"Real friends send Snaps." - Snapchat

Snapchat is a multimedia messaging app launched in 2011, known for its unique feature of disappearing messages and photos. It allows users to send photos and videos that vanish after being viewed, adding a layer of privacy and spontaneity to social interactions.

Key features include Stories, where users can post content for 24 hours, a wide array of fun filters and lenses, and Discover, which provides curated content from publishers and creators. Snapchat has become a cultural phenomenon, particularly among younger users, redefining how we communicate.

The app aims to empower users to express themselves in creative and fun ways, connecting them with friends through multimedia experiences.
