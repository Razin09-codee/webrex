About Steam

"For gamers, by gamers." - Steam

Steam is the largest digital distribution platform for PC gaming, offering a vast library of games across all genres. Launched by Valve Corporation in 2003, Steam has evolved into a vibrant community and marketplace for gamers and developers worldwide.

With features like social networking, cloud saves, achievements, and mod support, Steam enhances the gaming experience for its users. It also serves as a crucial platform for indie developers to distribute their games to a global audience.

Steam remains a go-to destination for PC gamers, providing tools, content, and community resources that continue to shape the gaming industry.
