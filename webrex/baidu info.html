About Baidu

"Innovation distinguishes between a leader and a follower." - Steve Jobs

Baidu is a Chinese multinational technology company specializing in Internet-related services and products, including a popular search engine. Founded in 2000 by Robin Li and Eric Xu, it has become one of the largest AI and internet companies in the world.

Baidu offers various services such as online marketing, cloud services, and autonomous driving technology, making significant investments in artificial intelligence research and development.

Its search engine dominates the Chinese market, similar to Google in other parts of the world.
