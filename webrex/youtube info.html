About YouTube

"Broadcast Yourself." - YouTube

YouTube is the world's leading video-sharing platform, launched in 2005, allowing users to upload, share, and view videos. With billions of users globally, YouTube has transformed how content is consumed and created.

Key features include user-generated content, subscriptions to channels, live streaming, and a wide range of video genres from vlogs to music videos. YouTube also offers monetization options for creators, enabling them to earn from their content.

The platform's mission is to give everyone a voice and show them the world through video.
