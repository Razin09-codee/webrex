About Jotti

"Scan your files for free with Jotti." - Jotti

Jotti is a free online malware scanning service that allows users to upload files to check for viruses and other malicious content. Utilizing multiple antivirus engines, Jotti provides a thorough examination to help keep your system safe.

The service is simple and user-friendly, designed for anyone needing quick malware detection without the hassle of installing software. Jotti helps ensure that files are clean and secure before downloading or executing them.

With Jotti, users can feel more confident about the safety of their downloads, making it a valuable tool in the fight against malware and cyber threats.
