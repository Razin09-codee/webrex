About Speedtest by Ookla

"The global standard in internet performance testing." - Speedtest

Speedtest by Ookla is the most recognized tool for measuring internet speed and performance. Founded in 2006, it provides users with the ability to test their download and upload speeds, ping, and overall network performance across various devices and platforms.

With millions of tests conducted daily, Speedtest empowers users to understand their internet service quality, helping them make informed decisions about their broadband plans and troubleshoot connectivity issues.

Speedtest also offers detailed insights and statistics on global internet speeds, making it a vital resource for consumers, businesses, and internet service providers.
