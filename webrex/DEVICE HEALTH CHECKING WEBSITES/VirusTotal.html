About VirusTotal

"Your security is our priority." - VirusTotal

VirusTotal is a free service that analyzes files and URLs to detect viruses, worms, trojans, and other kinds of malware. It aggregates many antivirus products and online scan engines to provide users with comprehensive security information.

By allowing users to upload files or submit URLs, VirusTotal provides insights into potential threats, making it a valuable tool for both individuals and organizations aiming to enhance their cybersecurity measures.

With a simple interface and detailed reports, VirusTotal helps users make informed decisions about the safety of their files and web pages, ensuring a more secure online experience.
