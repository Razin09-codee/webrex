About Speccy

"Get detailed insights into your system's specifications." - Speccy

Speccy is a free tool developed by Piriform that provides users with a detailed overview of their system's hardware and specifications. It displays critical information about your CPU, RAM, motherboard, graphics cards, and more in an easy-to-understand format.

With Speccy, users can quickly assess their system's performance and identify any potential issues. It's an essential tool for gamers, tech enthusiasts, and anyone looking to optimize their computer's capabilities.

The software is lightweight and user-friendly, making it accessible for users of all skill levels. Speccy helps you understand your computer better, allowing you to make informed decisions about upgrades and maintenance.
