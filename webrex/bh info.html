About Berkshire Hathaway Inc.

"The best investment you can make is in yourself." - Warren Buffett

Berkshire Hathaway Inc. is a multinational conglomerate holding company, headquartered in Omaha, Nebraska. It was founded by Warren Buffett, one of the most successful investors in history. The company owns a wide range of subsidiaries in industries including insurance, rail transportation, energy, manufacturing, retail, and more.

Under the leadership of Buffett, Berkshire Hathaway has grown into one of the largest companies in the world by revenue and market capitalization. The company is known for its long-term investment strategy and focus on acquiring businesses with strong fundamentals.

Key subsidiaries include GEICO, BNSF Railway, Berkshire Hathaway Energy, and several major industrial and consumer brands. Berkshire Hathaway is also a significant shareholder in companies such as Apple, Coca-Cola, and American Express.
