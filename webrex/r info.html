About R

R is a programming language and environment widely used for statistical computing, data analysis, and graphical representation. Developed by statisticians, R is powerful for a range of data science applications, including data manipulation, statistical modeling, and machine learning.

It is open-source and has a vast ecosystem of libraries and packages that extend its functionality. Researchers, data scientists, and statisticians rely on R for its flexibility and advanced statistical tools.

R is known for its robust plotting capabilities and has become a popular choice in academia and industry for data analysis.
