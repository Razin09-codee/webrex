About Groww

"Investing made simple and accessible for everyone" - Groww

Groww is an investment platform in India offering a user-friendly interface for individuals to invest in mutual funds, stocks, and digital gold. Founded with the vision to simplify investment for beginners, Groww provides tools and resources that make financial planning easy and transparent.

Through the Groww app and website, users can access diversified investment options, SIPs (Systematic Investment Plans), and portfolio insights, all aimed at empowering people to make informed financial decisions with minimal fees and clear, educational content.

With a focus on trust and simplicity, Groww is committed to providing a seamless investment experience for everyone, from beginners to seasoned investors.
