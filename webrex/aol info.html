About AOL

"The internet's original home." - AOL

AOL (America Online) is a pioneering internet service provider and web portal that played a significant role in the early days of the internet. Founded in 1985, it became one of the most recognized brands in online services, known for its dial-up internet access and user-friendly interface.

AOL offers a range of services, including email, news, and entertainment content. Over the years, it has transitioned from a subscription-based service to a free web portal, providing users with access to a variety of online resources and tools.

AOL's mission is to deliver trusted content and experiences, connecting users to the information they need in a rapidly evolving digital landscape.
